Meet O'Neil

O’Neil Fuller is a Certified Master HVACR Educator with over 20 years of experience in leading, designing, and advancing HVACR programs. He has taught at top institutions like Kroger, Tulsa Welding/HVAC School, and Georgia Piedmont Technical College. His expertise spans curriculum development, energy efficiency, refrigeration, and emerging technologies.

He is certified by NATE, ICE, EPA, and HVAC Excellence, and he’s on a mission to modernize the HVACR trade — from the classroom to the job site, and even the bookshelf.
